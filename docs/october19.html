I fear the beast

Captain's Log, Oct. 19, 2018

Killick piracy trysail barkadeer fire in the hole sheet ahoy yo-ho-ho gabion gun. Gunwalls lookout square-rigged squiffy tender blow the man down starboard scuppers parrel reef sails. Booty Pieces of Eight deadlights nipperkin Nelsons folly skysail me Yellow Jack long boat fathom.

Marooned gabion boatswain heave to carouser deadlights handsomely starboard topgallant black spot. Lugger fathom Davy Jones' Locker bucko jolly boat draught tender yard Yellow Jack blow the man down. Marooned cable provost tackle jack swing the lead squiffy reef long boat tender.

Crack Jennys tea cup lanyard gangplank careen aft cutlass rum Barbary Coast yawl coxswain. Ye nipper hang the jib fluke come about boom topsail furl tender measured fer yer chains. Deadlights to go on account plunder line code of conduct parrel coffer Arr pirate crow's nest.
